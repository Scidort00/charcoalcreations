Charcoal Creations

About Us

I specialize in hand-drawn custom charcoal art that captures the perfect natural scenery for you. Our artworks range from $20 to $50 and are completed and delivered within 2 to 10 weeks, depending on the requested level of detail.

Donations

10% of the proceeds are donated to one of two oragnizations of your choice; the Audubon Society and Oceana. I believe in contributing to global efforts aimed at creating a better world with each purchase.

How to request

If you'd like to order an art piece, please email
charcoalcreations@proton.me. In the email please describe the basics of what you're looking for and we can discuss details and pricing.

Please note that all requested drawings must meet the criteria of plants, nature scenes, reptiles, and dinosaurs.
